Singapore Package

Travelling Singapore leaves you
speechless then turns you
into a storyteller.

Singapore Itinerary 4 Days / 3 Nights

  • Day 1: Arrival in Singapore

    On arrival in Singapore, get transferred to your hotel. On reaching, check-in to your hotel and spend the day at your leisure, exploring Singapore in your own way. Singapore is a model city, quite unlike any other in the world. The city is dynamic, colourful and offers a harmonious blend of culture, cuisine, art and architecture. Brimming with unbridled energy, this little South-East Asian dynamo embodies the finest of both the East as well as the West.

  • Day 2: Singapore

    Meals: Breakfast included.

    After a hearty breakfast at the hotel, gear up for an exciting half-day city tour of Singapore. The tour will commence with a drive around the Civic District past the Padang Cricket Club, the historic Parliament House, the Supreme Court, the City Hall and a short stop at Merlion Park for incredibly stunning views of Marina Bay.

    Don’t forget to click pictures with the Merlion, a mythological creature, part lion and part fish! Spend the evening at leisure or embark on a safari quite unlike any other ever seen, heard or experienced! It is Singapore's unique Night Safari (optional tour), the world's first wildlife park built for visits at night. Nestled amidst 40 hectares of lush green forests, it makes use of subtle lighting techniques thereby letting visitors view over 1,000 nocturnal animals belonging to more than 100 different species in their natural habitats. Return to the hotel and enjoy a comfortable overnight stay at your hotel.

  • Day 3: Singapore

    After Breakfast, day is free for personal activites. In the Evenning enjoy night safari. Overnight stay at Hotel.

  • Day 4: Singapore

    After enjoying breakfast, check-out of your hotel and proceed to the airport with memories unlimited!
