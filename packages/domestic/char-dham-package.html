Char Dham Package

Sacred places(dhams) which has been
visited by Gods themselves,
and after visiting all 4 of them
Humans can attain moksha/mukti. Gangotri,
Yamunotri,
Kedarnath,
Badrinath,

Char Dham Itinerary 11 Days / 10 Nights

  • Day 1: Arrival at Haridwar

    Our Driver will meet you then proceed to Haridwar on arrival check in to hotel to day free for leisure or join evening Ganga Aarti at Hari ki Pauri.

  • Day 2: Haridwar - Barkot

    Morning proceed to Barkot via Mussoorie. In route visit Kempty fall. On arrival check-in to hotel. Overnight stay in hotel with leisure at Barkot.

  • Day 3: Barkot - Yamunotri - back Barkot

    Morning proceed to Janki Chatti Trek starts from here to Yamunotri (6kms). Either by walk or by horse or by doli at own cost. Arrival Yamunotri, one can cook rice by packing it in a cloth and dipping it in the hot water of the hot kund. Pilgrims take this cooked rice home as "prasad". Here near the temple "pooja" can be offered to Divya Shila. After taking bath in Jamunabai kund's warm water and having "dashing" of pious "Yamunaji" returning to Hanuman Chatti. Later drive back to Barkot. Overnight stay in hotel at Barkot.

  • Day 4: Barkot - Uttarkashi

    Morning proceed to Uttarkashi. Check- in to the hotel. Later visit Vishwanath temple. Thereafter enjoy comfortable overnight at hotel in Uttarkashi.

  • Day 5: Uttarkashi- Gangotri - back Uttarkashi

    Morning proceed to Gangotri (3048mtrs), in route we enjoy picturesque Harsil village, Bhagirathi river and the most magnificent view of the Himalayas. After Gangotri darshan return to Uttarkashi & overnight stay in hotel.

  • Day 6: Uttarkashi – Sitapur (Nearby Gaurikund)

    Early morning proceed to Sitapur en route visit Tehri dam and Rudraprayag-Guptkashi. Check-in to hotel & overnight stay in hotel with leisure at Sitapur. ( Guest can take Helicopters between Guptkashi to Gourikund four place)

  • Day 7: Guptakashi/ Sitapur – Kedarnath- Guptakashi/ Sitapur

    Early morning proceed to Gaurikund, trek start from Gaurikund to Kedarnath (3548mts) on foot or on by pony/doli. Tour members should carry personal medicines, heavy woolen, toiletries and clothes for an overnight halt at Kedarnath. Visit Kedarnath temple. Evening back to Guptakashi/Sitapur for overnight stay.

  • Day 8: Guptkashi - Badrinath

    Morning proceed to Badrinath via Karnprayag. Check- in to hotel later visit to sightseeing spots like Mana,Vyas Gufa, Maatamoorti, Charanpaduka, Bhim Pund and the "Mukh" of the Saraswati River. Just within the 3 kms far from Badrinathjee latter back to Badrinath and Visit Badrinath temple for aarti late at evening. Overnight stay in hotel.

  • Day 9: Badrinath – Rudraprayag

    Early morning, pilgrims after having a bath in the Taptkund have the darshan of Badrivishal Brahamakamal is significant for pinddan shraddh of ancestors (pitrus).. Later proceed to Rudraprayag. On arrival check-in to hotel & overnight stay in hotel with leisure.

  • Day 10: Rudraprayag to Rishikesh

    Early morning proceed to Haridwar enroot visit to Laxman Jhula, Ram Jhula & Thirteen Story Temple, Swarg Ashram. Later proceed to Haridwar check into hotel overnight stay at hotel.

  • Day 11: Haridwar/Rishikesh to Back Delhi

    After morning breakfast at hotel proceed to Delhi.
