Dharamshala Itinerary
3 Days / 2 Nights

  • Day 1: Delhi - Dharamshala

    Pick up from Delhi, meet and greet by our representative and then transfer to your commutes, reach at Dharamshala. Check in at your pre booked hotel. Enjoy your dinner. Overnight stay at hotel.

  • Day 2: Dharamsala

    After breakfast proceed for Dharamsala full tour. Proceed to visit Bhagsunag Fall: Bhagsunag Fall is located 11 kilometers away from Dharamshala town and has an ancient temple, a fresh water spring, many waterfalls and numerous slate quarries. Kunal Pathari: located three kilometers away from Dharamshala, Kunal Pathari is a temple of the local Goddess. After that visit the Mcleodganj. Return back to the hoter. After dinner, overnight stay at hotel.

  • Day 3: Dharamshala - Delhi

    After take breakfast check out from the hotel & then transfer to Delhi at your home.
